Frequently asked questions

Is there a limit on the project volume?

Yes. A project may not exceed 600 man hours.

Do you maintain externally created software?

No. If a central component is ours though, we might be able to agree on an incorporation and maintainance contract.

Do you offer coaching?

You can order employee coaching for our products. Contact us via support@kompound.solutions or within your personal customer service area.

How long do you support products?

There is no maximum time limit. We offer indiviual maintainance contracts. Generally our contracts include a maintainance duration of one year. Product defects covered and regulated by law are of course not affected.

How do I report a product deficiency?

Your personal customer service area offers you a separate page for reporting product defects. We will usually respond to any report within 24 hours. Please do not report any software or hardware issue via mail. The reponse time might increase.

Do you produce hardware yourself? What is the maximum batch volume?

If you order a solution that includes custom developed hardware, we will offer you a sample batch of up to 10 devices. Included are the full schematics as well, ready for mass production by any contract manufacturer.

Do you offer consulting services?

Consulting is not our core business area. If our multi-year experience can be of any help, give it a try and contact us.
